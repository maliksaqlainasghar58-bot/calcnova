Three steps between you and your answer.

Pick a mode

Choose Basic for everyday arithmetic, or Scientific to unlock trigonometry, logarithms, powers, and roots.

02

Tap the keys to build your calculation, for example 2 + 2, or sin(30) for scientific functions.

Frequently asked questions

Is CalcNova free to use?

Yes. CalcNova is completely free, works in your browser, and requires no signup or download.

Does CalcNova work on mobile phones?

Yes. CalcNova is fully responsive and works smoothly on phones, tablets, and desktops.

What functions does the scientific calculator support?

CalcNova supports basic arithmetic, trigonometric functions (sin, cos, tan), logarithms, square roots, powers, factorials, and constants like pi and e.

Is my data stored anywhere?

No. All calculations run locally in your browser. CalcNova doesn't store or transmit your inputs.
